About Rosa Schuurman

Rosa is ex-cmd'er. She is a creative developer and has worked at different companies and now also has her own company called Vitrinekast. She's going to talk about creative ways to develop going beyond a website and her hobby called circuit bending.

Summary of Rosa's lecture

Today's lecture was all about circuit bending. It's something born in the 70's where people take a random circuit from an electronic device. They 'bend' the device till some strange sound comes out of if. Anyone can start doing it. You do not need a lot of experience to start. A few tips she gave: Use battery only devices, look for ways to short circuit the board and create a form of interaction with the board. lastly she gave a few tips about finding a good environment where you can join groups in different cities.

Conclusion: what I learned

There are many creative ways to think about tech. One of them is circuit bending, which i never heard of before. It sound interesting. But i don't think it's for me.
